About Me

Hello! My name is Frank Metcalf and I am 22 years old. I grew up in a western suburb of Chicago, Western Springs. I am the youngest of three siblings. I grew up with two older brothers, Anthony and Philip (28 & 26), my mom Cindy and my dad Phil.

Currently, I am senior at the University of Iowa studying Finance & Business Analytics and Information Systems. At Iowa, I am a tour guide where I give 90-minute tours to prospective students and families around campus and field any questions and concerns they may have. I am also a part of Moneythink. In this club, I go to surrounding Iowa City high schools and teach basic finance concepts (what a credit card is, how to buy a car, savings vs checking accounts). I also am a fan of intramurals, joining pickleball, sand volleyball, and basketball.

I will graduate from Iowa in May 2025. After graduation, I will return to Chicago and in August start working as a Credit Analyst at the Bank of Montreal. I am excited to put to use both my finance and business analytics skills that I have learned and curated at Iowa.

For fun, I love to travel. I just recently returned from Punta Cana and my second semester sophomore year I studied abroad in Italy and traveled to 10 different countries (see full list below). This summer I will be returning back to Europe to visit Denmark, France, Monaco, and Italy. I also enjoy watching movies, listening to music (specifically country, pop, and oldies), and going to the gym.
